About Ecolux:

Ecolux Electrical Co. Pvt. Ltd. has a long and impressive history in the business of lighting and electrical products. In short, our business experience encompasses import, export, distribution, and R&D for suppliers. This particularly applies to indoor and outdoor lighting fixtures, garden lights, switches, sockets, as well as protection devices like MCB and MCCB, among others.
What's more, Ecolux Electrical Co. Pvt. Ltd. is the exclusive home of some of the world's premium brands and products in Pakistan, including names like Panasonic, Fumagalli, Lutec, Venus, Tecmar, and more.
We are Ecolux Group, and we take pride in providing excellent quality branded products and services of superior quality, ultimately improving the lives of the people. People are at the heart of our company. We find our strength in trust, diversity, and progress. We stand by our values: a passion for quality, an enjoyment of life, and respect for people.

Panasonic Switches and Sockets

Panasonic Switches and Sockets: Panasonic is a world-leading manufacturer of electrical switches and sockets with more than 100 years of manufacturing history. Panasonic assures safety and comfort for end users, complying with IEC, BS, SASO, and EQM standards.
Moreover, sheets are available in various design concepts, including basic white and metallic refined colors, with a screw-less design. Therefore, they are suitable for all types of today's living and working environments.
